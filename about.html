I'm Erin. Nice to meet you.

Ebenwalk is the concatenation of my name. Eben Walk, little known to me at the time of creating my profile and website, is actually a sort of seasonal, natural event whereby the Eben Ice Caves are formed in the Hiawatha National Forest in Michigan.

But enough about that. I started learning code a while ago. I was working in a quiet museum digitizing their collection. The software didn’t do exactly what I wanted it to do and my work was seen by only a select few colleagues within the museum. I wanted to fix that and I wanted the museum to be able to share all my work and their collection with the greater public.

I began to tinker, read all the books, taking all the courses, attending every webinar, and listening to every podcast I could. I would have never imagined myself here, yet, here I am. Building things, creating applications, developing programs! To check out what I’ve built and what I’ve learned, head on over to my projects page.
